## Pro Tip: Paper Trail Emails

After every phone call with landlord or Wunderflats, send them an email immediately:

> "Following our call today at [time], you confirmed that: [summary of what they said]. Please let me know if I have misunderstood anything."

This converts verbal statements into written evidence. They almost never correct it, which means the summary stands.
