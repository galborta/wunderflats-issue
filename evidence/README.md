# Evidence Preservation — Do This Now

> Legal disputes are won on documentation. The single most important thing you can do right now, before anything else, is preserve the voicemail and all booking records.

---

## 🔴 URGENT — Do These Within the Next Hour

- [ ] **Save the landlord's voicemail to 3 locations:**
  - Your phone (do NOT delete, do NOT let it auto-expire)
  - Email the audio file to yourself at a second email address
  - Upload to Google Drive / iCloud / Dropbox

- [ ] **Screenshot your Wunderflats booking confirmation** (full page showing: dates, landlord, property, amount paid, booking reference number)

- [ ] **Download your Wunderflats payment receipt(s)** — PDF if available

- [ ] **Download the signed lease PDF** — save to the same folder

- [ ] **Screenshot all platform messages** between you and the landlord on Wunderflats

## 📋 Document Your Situation in Writing (today)

Write down while fresh:
- Exact content of the voicemail (what was said, in what order)
- Time and date of your Wunderflats support call, name of agent if you have it
- Your current accommodation situation (where are you staying from June 4?)

## 💶 Financial Evidence (from June 4 onward — keep everything)

- [ ] Hotel/Airbnb booking confirmation and receipts
- [ ] Any storage costs
- [ ] Extra transportation (Metro, taxi, Uber — screenshot receipts)
- [ ] Moving costs if you have to move twice

---

## 📧 Create a Paper Trail for Every Phone Call

After EVERY call with the landlord, his wife, or Wunderflats, immediately send an email:

> *"Following our call today at [TIME], you confirmed that: [SUMMARY OF WHAT WAS SAID]. Please let me know if I have misunderstood anything."*

This converts verbal admissions into written evidence. They rarely correct it. Silence = acceptance.
