# Conciliation Request Template

> **Cost: Free. Time: 1–3 weeks. Required: No (you can go straight to référé), but it's fast pressure.**

---

## How to File

**Online (fastest):** https://www.justice.fr/simulateurs/saisine-conciliateur
**In person:** Tribunal judiciaire de Paris, 4 boulevard du Palais, 75001 Paris

Bring or upload:
1. Signed bail mobilité
2. Proof of payment
3. Voicemail transcript/recording
4. Your mise en demeure + proof of sending (tracking number / email)
5. Any response (or non-response) from landlord and Wunderflats
6. All alternative accommodation receipts
